Hi my name is Tyler and I have some advice for you!

Take a breath

There will be a point (more than one!) where what you're doing has you fuming and ready to tear out your hair. That's a good point to walk away and take a few breaths. It's natural, it's normal, and it happens to everyone.

Don't be intimidated

Many people treat "computers" like a collection of great mythical secrets; it doesn't have to be that way! Take it step by step and forget what you know as an adult; try to learn like you did when you were younger and didn't know any better. Do it, then do it again. Do it over and over. Sometimes follow the instructions; sometimes don't, just to see what happens, but always keep moving forward!

Always keep moving forward!

Way important is the Dori principle: "Just keep swimming!" Always make progress, even minor progress. Having trouble with your main class or functionality? Great, it happens; move on to something smaller that you know you can do, so you can get your groove back!
