Hi my name is Elyse and I have some advice for you!

Read All of the Directions

Fair warning, this might be silly advice, but I cannot tell you how many times I've been stuck halfway through a project only to realize that I just skimmed the directions.

It is so worth your time to read the entire document before ever opening your text editor

Fake it Til you Make it!

When I first started, I didn't think I was a 'techie.' What Skillcrush has shown me is that as long as you put in the work, you can be anything you want.

Every step of the way I have faked it, taken so many notes, then practiced until I felt comfortable. Maybe this isn't something everyone needs to do, but for this upcoming 'techie' it has made a huge difference

The internet is amazing!

While it might feel like a very new skill to you, the internet has been there, done that! Use guides, forums, customer service, anything you need to make you more knowledgable.
