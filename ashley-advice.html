Hi my name is Ashley and I have some advice for you!

Wear sunscreen.

Just do it, especially if you have tattoos.

Stick to one topic / language / book and see it through.

None of this jumping around business...keep pushing through, even if you get stuck or feel confused.

Celebrate your successes

Learning to program is a challenge; when you've made strides, acknowledge them! Remember, Rome was not built in a day.

Find a programming partner and work towards a common goal

Strength in numbers...plus, having a pal to ride the programming struggle bus with makes the ride less bad.
