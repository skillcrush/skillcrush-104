Hello my name is Tessa and I have some advice for you!

Devtools is your friend!

Learning to use devtools has completely changed how I solve coding conflicts

Once I finally figured out all of the ins and outs of devtools it greatly reduced the time it takes to fix issues

It reveals secrets

Devtools shows you all of the pesky preset browser styles that may conflict with your own styles.

It also allows you to see what code might be conflicting with another by crossing out styles that aren't able to apply & It tells you where each syle is coming from.

I highly suggest learning dev tools!
