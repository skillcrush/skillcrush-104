Hi my name is Kathy and I have some advice for you!

Your Age is Irrelevant

Don't ever let anyone tell you that you are too young or too old to begin a new journey.

That is what education is, a new journey. You can change your life at any age.

Never give up.

Learn to embrace the things that stump you. When you get through them and figure them out you will have learned valuable lessons and feel elated.

Push yourself

Always push yourself further than you think you can go. Try new things. You cannot know if a programming language is going to thrill you until you dig in and go for it.
