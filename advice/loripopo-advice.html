Reflect and Celebrate

Embarking on the journey to code is frustrating. It's probably unlike anything you have done before. Allow yourself to get frustrated, but don't forget to celebrate what you have accomplished. Take time to reflect on what you have learned. Document the frustrations and the subsequent triumphs. You'll be amazed at what you learned!
