Confusion is a sign that clarity awaits... oof. just over those really tall mountains

you might be doing something that you have never done before. It's a new challenge. a new adventure. and maybe even something that's never been done before. and dangit! it's hard. and tiring. and frustrating.

Never the less you should persist.

Put on your elizabeth warren t-shirt on and keep at it. This dangit! moment is a normal part of the process of learning a new awesome skill. the most critical part of the process is proving to yourself that you can and will persist. PLUS! the view from the top of those really tall mountains is a sweet reward.
