Hi, my name is James and I have some advice for you!

Carpe diem!

What? Crappy dimes? No, carpe diem! It means "seize the day" in Latin.

If an opportunity comes up, and you want to take it, then take it! Especially if you have some idea already of what your expenses are.

Life is short.

We only get a certain amount of time on this Earth, so we need to make it memorable with the resources we have.

Apply for that dream job even if you don't get it. Go on a cruise in the Bahamas. Explore a new part of the world, or try octopus! Whatever you consider new and exciting, go for it! Because you never know when you won't have that chance again.
