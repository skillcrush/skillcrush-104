Hi my name is Bartleby Willard and I have some advice for you!

Everything works to the good of those that love the Lord who are the called according to the Lord's purpose

What does that mean? Do only those who are called according to the Lord's plan love God and enjoy the resulting eventual complete success?

God can't be like that.

Who are you, but a human, to say how God can and cannot be?

If I have no insight into the divine, how can I know which religious ideas to follow and how to interpret them?

Did the author make a mistake? Or am I mistaken about God? Or am I misunderstanding the author's meaning?

Oh, see, that one was Paul; by his own admission (1 Corinthians 13), he doesn't always get everything right. Of course, I admit the same of myself. So, huh; impasse I guess.
