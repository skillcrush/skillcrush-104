Hi my name is MacKayla and I have some advice for you!

Don't Underestimate Yourself

Coding may seem intimidating in the beginning, but it's not as difficult as it seems.

Break things into small steps if you feel overwhelmed by the amount there is to learn. Take your time, you can do it!

Walk Away When You Feel Frustrated

When you can't solve something and you feel like you're about to throw your computer through the window, walk away for a while.

Give youself some time to relax, then come back to your project and ask for help

Have Fun

Don't forget to enjoy the process of leanring something new!

Show off your accomplishments to your friends and family, be proud of yourself and what you've learned
