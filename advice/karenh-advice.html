#1 Your Going to Think You Suck! ALL THE TIME

Its so exciting thinking about all the fun creative applications and sites you can code up. You see your favorite site and think wow I want to learn how to do that, so you seek a way to get educated.

You begin to play around with basic code and think oh this is not so bad. Then you start connecting the dots and things seem a little iffy. Your other peers seem to pick up techniques faster than you and so doubt starts to wave its wittle hand. "Can I handle this?" you begin to question.

Dont worry! This is all apart of the process. Doubt and fear are normal and feeling like you dont know enough is almost a requirement.

So when you feel like you suck and arent sure you can code. I advise you to push through and challenge yourself and prove yourself wrong.

Because next year when you are coding like a beast and snapping those if statements into place you are going to be one badass coder!
