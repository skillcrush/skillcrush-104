Hi my name is Jamie and I have some advice for you!

Go SLOW!

When I started Skillcrush I wanted to zip through everything so I could finish and be a tech wiz asap!

I quickly learned that this was the wrong mindset!

You can't "gloss over" anything in your courses because you will definately need that knowledge down the road.

Everything is important and relates to everything else!

  • Go slow! Take your time with each and every task - there are no shortcuts.
  • Read and re-read everything (a lot of times).
  • Watch and re-watch and re-watch videos!
  • Stop when you don't understand something! Don't move on! You will come across the same issue again - guaranteed!
  • Use as many resources as you can to help you understand something before moving on.
  • Don't be afraid to start something over! It can only help give you a deeper understanding and more practice.
  • Stay motivated and always reflect on the tremendous amount of new skills that you've already obtained!
  • Never stop learning!
