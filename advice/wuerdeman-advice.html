Hi my name is D'Arcy Wuerdeman and I have some advice for you!

Failing can be the best thing to happen

In the last 2 months since starting this Blueprint I have gotten used to failing and getting confused in certain projects. However, it is the best thing that has happened for me. I have been able to play around with code and figure out what works and what doesnt.

I truly believe that failing and getting confused teaches the problem solving skills needed for tech.

What to do when you just can't anymore?

Sometimes you just can't look at your code and computer screen anymore. Everything, looks the same and you're just down right annoyed. I have found that taking a break, even a full day of rest away from what you have been working on, can help you refresh.

It's truly amazing to see how fast you can fix a bug, when you give yourself some space to regroup.
