Hi my name is Bryan Herbert and I have some advice for you!

Simply don't give up!

There may be instances where you question yourself and your abilities. Remember, though, you are learning something completely new. Just stick with it!

Remember, we don't grow when things are easy. We grow when facing challenges. Coding can certainly have it's moments. :)

Get help when needed!

Sometimes, you may be overwhelmed with an obstacle. Asking someone else can take a weight off your shoulders.

We have a support team around us, wanting us to succeed.

If pride or nerves keep you from asing questions in desperate times, try to move past that. Let's help each other out!
