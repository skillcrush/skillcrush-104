Know Your Space

When you start programming you are doing something you have never done before. It is best to start by being really familiar with how to navigate your PC.

It is very important to know how to navigate in and out of file folders and setting up a strcucture to work with.

Organization is Your Best Friend

Organization plays an important role. It is great to be as organized as possible and plan ahead. Think things through

Don't be afraid to experiment and try new things. Some of our best work comes from chances we are not afraid to take.
