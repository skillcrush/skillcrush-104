Hi my name is Jessi and I have some advice for you!

Don't just limit yourself to doing the classwork

Every moment you say to yourself "I wonder how that's done" is an opportunity to learn something valuable. The excitement is there; the drive to learn something. The resources are endless. Take every bit of that curiosity seriously and see it through.

Confusion, therefore, is a sign that you are heading in the right direction. It's a signal that you have engaged in the problem solving pursuit that is math and technology and that it's time for you to dig deeper and keep going because clarity awaits.

Let go of the feeling that you are an imposter.

It holds you back more than you realize.
