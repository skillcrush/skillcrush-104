Perfect Is The Enemy Of Good

When you start to code, you might have the thought that your site "isn't good enough" or "just a few more tweaks and it will be perfect."

So you delay and delay and put off "shipping" the project. Meanwhile your client (even if it's you) is getting impatient because they want to launch.

Remember websites are ALWAYS "works in progress." Better to launch something that can be tweaked and evolved continuously, than to delay and produce nothing.
