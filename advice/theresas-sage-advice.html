Hi my name is Theresa and I have some advice for you!

It starts easy and gets tough fast!

But it's not impossible. There is a lot to learn and it won't happen overnight.

You are in this for the long haul. Keep at it and keep asking questions.

Fake it till you make it!

It takes a while to really understand what is happening when you write code and why it does what it does. Then one day, maybe, it falls in to place.

This is a normal part of the process of learning a new technology, and in fact, it's a critical part of the process.

You see when you encounter a new programming language part of your learning process includes learning to train your eye. The way to train your eye is to read a good deal of code even when you don't totally understand what it's doing.
