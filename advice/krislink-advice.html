Hi my name is Kris and I have some advice for you!

Don't doubt your abilities because of mistakes, even if there are many!

Most often, mistakes are easily fixed and are not the end of the world. When I was learning responsive design I had many errors and it took me longer than expected to finish. I began to think that this was not for me and that I may not ever finish! Once I got through the parts that were difficult, it started coming really quick for me and finally clicked!

Give it time and be patient.

It takes a lot of time to learn, and some things will come quick and easy. Other things may take a little while! Take your time and check through your work carefully and reread or rewatch videos to make sure you didn't miss anything.
