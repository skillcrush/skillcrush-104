Hi! My name is Vicki G and I have some advice for you!

Never give up!

Learning a new language is hard. Learning your first programming language just might be harder. But if you keep on trying, it will begin to make sense.

Ask for help.

When you get stuck (and you will!) ask for help. Google it; search stackoverflow.com; ask your group; ask your instructor. Do something!

This is a normal part of the process of learning a new technology, and in fact, it's a critical part of the process.

Don't just do something; sit there!

On the other hand, sometimes it's best to spend a little time just mulling over a topic. Take the time to understand the concepts you're learning. The Skillcrush guidelines of 3-4 weeks per course are just that — guidelines.
