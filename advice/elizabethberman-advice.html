Hi, my name is Elizabeth, and I have some advice for you!

Never give up; never surrender!

You may not be able to solve every problem right away. But you can always apply your problem-solving techniques.

That includes asking for help if you get stuck! Unlike other courses, you’re not on your own with Skillcrush. You have professional developers who are there to guide you every step of the way.

Don’t be afraid to fail

Screwing up is inevitable. You’ll make a typo. You’ll forget some essential bit of syntax (like putting single quotes around your @font-face font).

It’s not a big deal. You won’t break the internet. Just keep working the problem as above, and ask for help if you need it.
