I Love Getting Confused!

When first starting out with Skillcrush, I was having so much fun. I still am now, but I am starting to get to points in lessons where I often get super confused and overwhelmed.

But that confusion is really important because it allows me to have to go back and figure out where I went wrong. And most the time it's a stupid minor error that I overlooked. But all in all, at least I learned from it, right?

Slowing Down When Needed

To be honest, most of the time I'm stoked on being able to learn this new skill. I honestly didn't think I would be able to.

However it can get overwhelming when having to juggle classes with work, so I slow down when I need to, in order to not get too overwhelmed.
