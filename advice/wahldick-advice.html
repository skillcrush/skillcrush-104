Surround yourself with supportive and awesome people

Don't go at anything alone. Even if you don't have large community of fellow code queens at your disposal, make sure to have people close to you cheering you on.

Lean into the difficulty

If it were easy, everyone would be doing it. You're already here and clearly possess the grit to get after it. Keep plugging away and enjoy the challenge.

Do whatever you can to keep your curiosity. Do passion projects. Figure out how to make ideas realities.
