advice...

learning

is a

journey

go exploring.

Don't be afraid to try new things
& see what's out there!

Experiment with your code
& see what happens.

Look at other people's code
& test it out for yourself.

Be curious and keep learning
& trying new things.

retrace your steps.

When you get stuck,
go back to where you were when you got lost
& try again.

Getting stuck is like
taking the wrong fork in the road.

Sometimes you have to backtrack a little
in order to move forward

It can be super frustrating,
but it usually makes for a great story!

stop & look around

Every once in a while,
take a breath & look around.
Enjoy the view.

Things that used to be
confusing or frustrating
are probably much easier now.

Take time to look around
& see how far you've come.

so, get out there & turn

goals

into

milestones
