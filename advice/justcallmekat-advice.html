Hi there, I'm Kat!

I'm going give you some advice

Don't be a know-it-all

Don't drive yourself crazy trying to "know everything"; that's impossible and will only lead to frustration, fatigue, and worse of all burnout.

You're not google

Every developer, and I do mean EVERY DEVELOPER, uses google. So have mercy on yourself; be loving to yourself, and google it.

Team is the dream

Get involved in a developer's community as soon as you make the decision to start coding. It's the quickest way to learn, share, and grow.
