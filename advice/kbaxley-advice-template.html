Hi my name is Krysten and I have some advice for you!

Mistakes Happen

Part of learning how to write code is making mistakes. Learn from them.

Use your mistakes as a chance to learn. I find it helpful to research for the answer before reaching out to the group. I feel like I learn better that way.

Take a Break

When you get discouraged to a break.

Walk away from your work.

When you return, you may see something you were missing before.
