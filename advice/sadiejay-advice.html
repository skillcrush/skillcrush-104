Hi, I'm Sadie - this is advice to my earlier self! ♥

Don't Be Afraid to Ask for and Seek Help!

All programmers google things from time to time. It's perfectly normal and probably more effecient than trying to guess for hours.

When in doubt, google.

Engage in Community

When learning something new, old, and in between, having a community to learn with is key. It takes the pressure off of having to feel like you can only turn to yourself when stressed, confused, or even celebratory.

This is a normal part of the process of learning, and in fact, it's a critical part of the process.

You are not alone! You can do this!
