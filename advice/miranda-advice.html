Hi my name is Miranda and I have some advice for you!

1.Patience is Critical

Particular when you’re learning something new it is so easy to get impatient. Learning can be hard and it often takes a lot of time. However, that’s not a bad thing.

I believe that a lot of this impatience comes from a persistent myth that says if you don’t learn something fast than you’re not good at it. I think that myth is holding so many of us back from accomplishing what we dream of.

So don’t worry about proving that you’re good enough but putting pressure on yourself to learn super fast. Patiently put in the time and you can learn just about anything.

2.Take a Look Back

Every time I feel discouraged about how slowly I feel like I am moving through my life or how behind I think I am I take a look back.

I think about where I was 6 months ago, a year ago or even five years ago and I am always amazing by how much I have accomplished.

After realizing how far I have come I always feel much more confident that 6 months from now I will have made great progress, even if I feel very stuck right now.

Good Luck! : )
