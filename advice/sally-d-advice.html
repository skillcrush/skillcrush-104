Hi! My name is Sally D and I have some advice for you!

Remember - If it was Easy - Anyone Could Do It!


To all the wonderful newbies - you are embarking on a fabulous journey that will get you into the exciting world of tech. The job opportunities are great, the job flexibility is even better and there will always be new innovations for you to learn. You are on the right path.


Tech is extremely detailed work. You will be amazed how one missing letter will mess up the remainder of your code. Or forgetting to add a closing tag. So you need a great teacher - and you've found it in Skillcrush! Do the lessons, take notes, attend the office hours and if you have a question, add it to the Google Groups. You will be confused but power through it until you understand it. And tell yourself - if it was easy, anyone could do it.



Finish Strong!


There will be frustrating days and times you just want to sit down and cry. You may even be tempted to give up this journey. But don't. Choose now to finish strong. You have all the help you need available to you from the Skillcrush instructors. Use it! May all your tech dreams come true!
