Don't Give Up!

Keep moving forward even when it doesn't make sense.

If you're feeling lost, remember that you don't have to understand everything right away. Experiment a little. Look at someone else's example. Try an answer you have found when googling.

Now, you can try and understand why it worked and how you can make it better.

Don't be afraid to ask questions when you get stuck.

The answer might be something quite simple that you overlooked.

If you get really discouraged, go back and redo some things you've done before.

When you return to a previous problem, you will be surprised at how much you have learned since you first tried it. Things that were confusing will be clear now.

Now, attack your current problem with some new confidence!
