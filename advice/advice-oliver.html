Hi my name is Oliver and I have some advice for you!

Patience is a virtue

When you start coding, be patient, be patient, be patient.

Some things might not make sense at all, but read through the work, listen and watch the videos with glee, and make lots of notes.

Review the work.

When reviewing and doing the class exercises, you will be glad to learn that things will start making sense.

Keep at it and keep a positive attitude

Celebrate every milestone achieved. Believe it or not, YOU ARE BUSY CODING woop woop!!!!!
