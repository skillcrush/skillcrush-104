Hi my name is Jenn Coyle and I have some advice for you!

Remember Your Why

When learning code, never forget your why. Why are you learning to code? What obstacles are you trying to get past?

For me, I am learning how to code because I want to be able to spend more time with my son, Beans.

Persist

It isn't going to be easy all the time. It will take months or years before you feel fully comfortable with your new skills. But this level of discomfort is a good thing; it means you're learning and growing and bettering yourself as a developer.

As soon as you start to feel static, then you've stopped growing. Keep moving. Persist. Stay uncomfortable.
