Hi my name is Kim

I have some advice for you!

Confusion is NOT your friend - FOCUS!

In the beginning I should have listend to others and focused on one project at a time and I probably would be further along in my professional IT freelance journey.

Find Your Niche.

You cannot be a master of everything. Figure out what you like about this field and pursue it.

Nothing is CONSTANT but CHANGE.

This is true in the software field. There is always a shiny new object. Be careful not to reach for it if it does not meet your needs that are defined by your goals.

GOALS guide YOU - YOU create GOALS

Determine your goals and if everything you do is not inline with your goals either re-think your goals or don't do the task.
