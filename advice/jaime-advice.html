Learn at Your Own Pace

I've learned that it takes me a little longer than some people to get new ideas and concepts to stick. It can be daunting when you're in a class with others who seem to get it quicker and move on faster than you do. Just remember you learn at you're own speed and never mind everyone else!

Manage your time

Everyone has the same 24-hours in a day. Whether you spend that on Facebook, watching Netflix, or learning to code so you can advance your career - it's all up to you.
