Hi my name is Dani and I have some advice for you!

There is no such thing as a silly question!

It's likely that someone has gone through the exact same problem as you before, so there's no need to stew over it for hours.. ask the question!

Go away and come back...

Sometimes moving away from the screen and coming back to it with fresh eyes can be really helpful. I've had many eureka moments away from the computer!

Don't get overwhelmed!

Sometimes if I've looked too far ahead into the 'coding world' I've become very overwhelmed with how little I know and how far I've got to go. Take each step at a time, focusing on the task at hand and you will make the progress! Everyone was a beginner at one time :)
