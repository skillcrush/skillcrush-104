Hi, my name is Ashlyn, and I have some advice for you!

It takes time.

When you start coding your first website, you are doing something that you have never done before. It's a new challenge and a new opportunity.

It's going to take time to get where you want to be. There are no shortcuts for learning the information and no alternatives for doing the work.

Don't stay frustrated.

It's easy to become frustrated when you can't figure out how to write your code so that it does what you want it to do. The important thing is that you don't let yourself stay frustrated.

There are a multitude of resources at your disposal, and a lot of them are free. Don't be afraid to ask for help.
