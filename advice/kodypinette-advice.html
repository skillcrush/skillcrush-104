Hi my name is Kody Pinette and I have some advice for you!

Focus on building something cool!

When you first start out there will be a lot to take in. Have fun with it! Coding is all about building things!

The best thing about coding is looking at the changes and final product. There's no better feeling than looking at a webpage and thinking "I built that".

Pay attention to the little things

Sometimes the smallest thing like a missing end bracket or a typo can break your code. If you're stuck and cant figure out why something won't work take a break and come back with "fresh eyes" a lot of the time the error sticks out like a sore thumb
