Hi my name is Sofia and I have some advice for you!

Have a snack box!

We need a LOT of brain food to get through these lessons. I had it handy to have a snack box right next to me to help me through the challenges.

It's low key a dengerous idea because you would be eating all the time, but we need energy to learn something new!


Reading articles and forums and checking the class slack reaffirms that everyone is figuring it out just like you

Ever think, man, am I the only one who doesn't get it? Or feel like "yikes" whenever you don't get it right the first time?

As long as you keep hammering away and still try to look for answers, it's totally normal to feel like you SHOULD be getting it but you're not.
