Newbie Advice

Learning coding and programming is learning a NEW language.

Be kind to yourself, it is a Process. It is like riding a bike — challenging at first, but great once you get the hang of things!

Fun fact: If you don’t like something, you can change it. Coding is fun, creative and challenging.

Remember: Everyone started from the same place you are today.

If your project is getting the best of you, take a break. sometimes walking away will give you a fresh perspective.

There is always something to learn Stop over analyzing &

When you learn, share. There will always be someone that has coded longer, knows more tricks, and will be happy to help you along your way - find your tribe.

Open your mind, arms, and heart to new things and people, we are united in our differences.

Life is about the people you meet, and the things you create with them so go out and get stared creating. Network! join the groups who enjoy the code you do!

If it does not nourish your soul, get rid of it. The world needs you.

Web Development is for ANYONE at ANY AGE.

Forgive yourself ‐ Rome was not built in a day - Be kinder to yourself. Be adaptable. Stay curious. Never stop learning and exploring. Nothing works unless you do.

Never apologize for being your unique self. Be Kind, People will never forget how you have made them feel. Authenticity and confidence are sexy.

Show Gratitude. Your kindness has a ripple effect. Even Bill Gates or Steve Jobs didn't get it at one time (surely).

You only fail when you give up- don't let coding languages or errors frustrate you or let you feel stupid - remind yourself how far you come

What you seek is seeking you. Live your dream and share your passion. YOU DON'T NEED THEIR APPROVAL! - ask questions - and help where you are able!
