Hi, my name is Cathy Ferrell and I have some advice for you!

Try It On Your Own!

As I followed along with the videos and lessons and cheatsheets and quizzes some times it certainly felt that I had no idea if I was absorbing any of it!

Each week I set my self up with a small (i.e. achievable) project to practice what I had just learned.

Most of the time I surprised myself!

I was able to handle most of the work from memory and while some things took a number of tries - and a few google searches - I got to where I needed to go.

This confirmed for me that I was grasping the concepts and gave me the confidence to continue on with the next week's work!

And then I took a break!
