Hi my name is Kimberlie and I have some advice for you!

Take it easy on yourself!

I didn't realize how slow the proccess of learning would be!

My main advice would be to be patient with yourself

Don't forget to ask for help!

I get so frustrated with how slow my progress is and tend to forget to use the most important resource out there: other people!!

Most people like to help others and it helps them reaffirm their knowledge by teaching you so don't feel bad about asking for help.

Programming can be frustrating but at the end of the day it is very satisfying to see your sweat and tears come to fruition
