Ways to Stay On Track


Classroom Portability- Take your lessons on the go with a tablet!

Code Everyday- Encorporate your pursuit of learning how to code into your daily routine.

Walk Away- Go for a breather when you find yourself becoming frustrated.


Remember Why You Started


It'll be easy to put off learning a whole new set of skills.

It would be so much easier to take the day off to just lounge around.

Whenever you're feeling unmotivated, just remember exactly what it was that got you started.
