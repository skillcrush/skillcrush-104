Hi my name is Christine and I have some advice for you!

Don't get discouraged!

Most things won't work on the first try! If they did, it wouldn't be a challenge.

You will have to be a problem-solver. You will have to try out different solutions.

You don't have to have full understanding to make things happen.

Learn as you go. Whether you know how to do something or not, you can figure it out and you can do it.

This is a normal part of the process of learning a new technology, and in fact, it's a critical part of the process.
