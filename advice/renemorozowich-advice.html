Random Advice

My first piece of advice is -- don't worry. You'll figure it out. Sometimes it helps to talk it out. Out loud. To another human. Away from the computer.

Fake it til you make it. A lot of people don't like this advice, but I think it can be valuable. Have some confidence, and you'll rise to the occasion. If you don't have confidence in yourself, other people won't have confidence in you.

Six Stages of Debugging

This is funny because it's true.

  1. That can't happen.
  2. That doesn't happen on my machine.
  3. That shouldn't happen.
  4. Why does that happen?
  5. Oh, I see.
  6. How did that ever work?
