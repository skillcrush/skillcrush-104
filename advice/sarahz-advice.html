What I choose to remember every day.

Each Obsticle is a Choice!

One of the most attractive things about coding for me was the availability of information while I was working. If I run into a problem and I need an answer, I'm already at my computer ready to find it.

You will never truly stop learning code. It is an ever evolving field that keeps you on your toes. So when you run into a problem that stumps you, its important to make the choice to actually LEARN how to fix it. Not just copy paste the answer.

Take a Breath, Give Yourself Time.

Running into a problem can be really frustrating when you're just starting out. Especially if you're really busy. But always try to stop, and take a breath, and give yourself some time to actually learn from your mistakes. You deserve to improve!

There is nothing wrong with copying and pasting code to create a shortcut, just be sure you understand how the shortcut works before you use it.

I always say to myself, "yes, you're busy. But you are never too busy to work on yourself. You deserve the satisfaction of understanding the solution."
