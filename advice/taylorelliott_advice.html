Hi my name is Taylor, and I have some advice for you!

Ask lots of questions!

  • No matter what the question is, odds are, it needs to be asked, and you probably aren't the only one looking for the answer.
  • And in that vein, don't worry about feeling silly because no one knows everything all the time.

Take breaks!

  • Stepping away from the computer screen is a huge part of actually seeing what you're doing. And on a literal level, it's important for the eyeballs.
