Hi Newbies! My name is Marie and I have some advice for you!

Don't Give Up!

Sometimes you will get stuck and want to bang your head into the keyboard trying to make something work. This would happen to me sometimes when figuring out breakpoints in responsive design. Take a deep breath, take a break and then go back to it. Most of the time you find your solution shortly there after.

Use your skillcrush google groups to ask questions. There is no such thing as a stupid question! I also find I can get find solutions to my questions on stackoverflow.com and w3schools.com.

Stay Focused!

Remember why you are learning to code and set goals for yourself. For me it was to transition to a new job where I can apply my web development skills. I focused to finish my portfolio to showcase the HTML and CSS skills I have already learned to hopefully land a new role utilizing those skills while finishing up the program and diving into javascript. Good Luck and Happy Coding!
