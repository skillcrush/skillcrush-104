Hi my name is Kristian and I have some advice for you!

Take it slow

When you're new to coding, it's important for you to take your time and make sure you understand everything you're learning.

Don't just follow the instructions, but try to understand why you're doing what you're doing, and how everything works.

It's ok to feel overwhelmed

You've entered a new universe of knowledge, and there's so much to learn. You're not expected to grasp everything immediately.

Take an approach that works for you. This could be learning in small chunks, or embedding yourself into it for hours in a row.

Above all, remember that you're not alone. There are plenty of helpful websites and forums where you can ask questions and get the answers you're looking for!
