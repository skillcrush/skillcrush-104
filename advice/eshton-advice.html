Hi my name is Eshton, and I have some advice for you!

Don't be so impatient!

I'm a very impatient person sometimes, and, especially when it comes to this blueprint, I never think I'm doing enough fast enough.

The thing is, the process is the destination.

There's no "end" to learning to code--or design for that matter. So sit back and let the journey unfold.
