Hello, my name is Hoyt and I will try and give some advice

Absence makes the heart grown fonder

If you're like me and don't have a lot of confidence in yourself it's easy to dislike your work, especially if you've been spending a lot of time on it. If it's starting to get to you take some time away from your project and let it simmer. You'll probably feel better about it when you come back to it later.

Move forward one step at a time

A good motivator is having something you want to do or make with code. Even if it's out of reach now, it still gives you purpose.

Another thing is to keep going little by little. Even if it feels like you're not making progress it adds up eventually. If you put it off it's easy to stop working altogether.

Practice makes proficient

Even if it's a bit of a dead horse it's worth repeating. The more you try something out the easier it will get. And if you're still struggling with a concept look up different tutorials online, sometimes it just takes someone describing it in the right way for it to click.
