You Don't Have to Know Everything Right Away

When I first started out, there were a ton of things I thought I "should" be able to do, but there is NO should!

"Just In Time" Learning in Fiiiiiine.

Everything you need to know, you can learn when you need it. It's okay to research as you go along, and to struggle to find things.

The more you seek to solve a specific problem, the better you'll understand the process as a whole. It's all part of the growth experience :)

You just have to have a little faith that you will find the answers and that, in the process of looking for those answers, you're picking up other really important knowledge. Nothing is a waste of time.

You just have to keep going, and keep looking for better and better ways to solve problems.
