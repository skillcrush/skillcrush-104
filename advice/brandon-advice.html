I have some advice for you!

We all start exactly where you're at.

Getting into the world of web and learning to code is not an easy journey. But remember that we all started in your shoes and becoming a software or web developer IS possible. Just give it time, patience, and lots of practice. You'll get there. (:

Confusion is key, it shows you are heading in the right direction. Frustration will happen but the satisfaction of solving problems and continuous learning you get to partake in every day makes the whole journey worth it.

You don't have to do this alone.

First off, let me just state the fact that you are working in the heart of what we define as "connectivity" in this world. The coding community is vast and full of answers. Don't ever think you need to figure this out on your own because this field thriving by thinking the opposite.

This is a normal part of the process of learning a new technology, and in fact, it's a critical part of the process.

You see when you encounter a new programming language part of your learning process includes learning to train your eye. The way to train your eye is to read a good deal of code even when you don't totally understand what it's doing.
