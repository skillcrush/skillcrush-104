Shutting Down the Fear of Failure

I have always chosen the cautious or "safe" path, but it has not always made me happy.

Often, this was done out of fear of failure, or fear that I would not stack up to expectations for myself, or of others.

But I have lived long enough to know that living in fear or in the "safe-zone" isn't going to get you very far.

Sometimes, you just have to try, and chances are, you will grow and become better from the experience. Very often, you will suceed in someway.

Also, take breaks and look at cute animals, corgis and kittens and stuff.
