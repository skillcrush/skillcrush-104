Hi my name is Jennifer Carver & I have some advice for you!

Start with why

Watch Simon Sinek's famous Ted Talk video Start With Why. Everyone has a why...
a reason to get up in the morning, a reason to exist, a purpose, cause or belief that inspires you
to do what you do. Figure out your 'why' and you'll have a much easier time
staying inspired and focused through the tough times.

Accomplishing things together
is better than going it alone

At one point, everyone was a beginner...everyone, so chances are good that problems
you encounter have been hashed out by thousands before you. Remember - we are all in this
together! Don't be too shy to reach out for support. In time you will come full circle
and you'll be the mama bird;-)

Come up for air

Sometimes we hit a wall and that's okay. Move on - go for a walk.
Grab a bite to eat. Have a nap. Start learning something new. Whatever you do,
just take a break. The answers will come when your mind is fresh.

Have faith

There will be many times when things will be about as clear as mud.
Stick with it, as you progress through the learning process and do things a few times,
it will start to make sense. Just trust.
