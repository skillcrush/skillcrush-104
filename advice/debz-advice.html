Hi my name is DebZ and I have some advice for you! WooHoo!

Feeling confused, lost, and dismayed? Yippee!!

That means one thing - you care about what your doing - which is the start of learning to get past the road blocks.

I usually stop doing what I'm frustrated at and distract myself.

Thats when I clean the bathroom sink or take out the recyclables or play coconut with Clover my dog...

Then after twenty minutes of doing something that I can simply be successful at - I go back and say "Just try..."

So hard - but try to suspend the judgement - things take time to sink in

It's like the knowledge has to marinate in your brain, soaking up the stuff and then organize it into a simple plan.

Sorry for the mixed metaphors....:)
