Hi my name is Zoe
and I have some advice for you!

Don't give up!

Sometimes it's going to be hard and you are going to feel like walking away but don't give up! I have definitely hit a few blocks along the way so far, doubted myself a lot, thought to myself maybe I am not clever enough to do this. But then I have to think, if it was easy everyone would be doing it!

So I have learned to not be so hard on myself, I can do it and I will do it. If I need a break I am going to take it and if I fall behind I am not going to panic or stress because I will catch up and I will get there and all of this will be worth it!

Look at how far you've come

Following on from my previous advice of not giving up, when things are tough, you are finding things difficult or even if you just have a spare minute, sometimes you just have to stop and see how far you have come. I look back and think wow I can do that and I learned how to do this, all in the space of a few weeks...that's pretty amazing!

So I like me, give yourself a big pat on the back for how far we've come and just think in another few weeks all the other cool things that we'll will be able to do!
