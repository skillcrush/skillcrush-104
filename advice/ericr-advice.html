Hi my name is Eric and I have some advice for you!

Code EVERYDAY

To get good at anything you need repition to build familiarity.

Unfortunately, unless you are a savant there is no substitution for time spent codeing.

Focus on Coding and take breaks to keep your mind fresh

Keep distractions to a minimum while coding and learning.

That said, take some breaks along the way to keep your mind fresh and nimble.

A 5 minute walk will refresh your mind and body and get you ready for hours and hours of coding
