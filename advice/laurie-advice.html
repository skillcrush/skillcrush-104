Hi, my name is Laurie and I have some advice for you!

  • When you start learning how to create websites, there's gonna be a lot of new information. But you just have to take it one info at a time.
  • It's ok to check back for the right code, element, property, spelling of anything. It helps you remember it later on.
  • Not everything makes sense the first time you see it, but trust that it works and eventually you'll undestand why.
  • Confusion is your friend!
  • Practice daily if possible. It's ok to take time off and enjoy life, but always come back to coding.
  • Ask questions, look for answers when you don't know something. The internet is an incredible source of information.
  • Don't be too hard on yourself. Nobody is perfect, and it's totally ok to make mistakes as long as you learn from them.
  • Experiment with your new knowledge and explore your new resources.
  • Don't give up!

And enjoy the journey!!!
