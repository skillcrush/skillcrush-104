One Step @ A Time

The best advice I can offer to anyone is take this journey one step at a time. When I initially started studying front-end development, it seemed like such a daunting task. I'm the type of person that needs to see a glimpse of the bigger picture, which meant I needed to know the skill sets and order of study that I should follow to be successful. This made this new pursuit even more intimidating.

Fortunately, Skillcrush, programs like it and online resources forced to focus on figuratively putting one foot in front of the other. Start with the core essentials and then proceed one layer at a time.
