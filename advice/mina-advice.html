When in doubt, google

The great thing about coding is that there's a wealth of support out there. A quick google search can always point you in the right direction, or at the very least to someone who's willing to help.

You know more than you think

Sometimes it can feel like information is just going in one end and out the other, but practice and you'll start to see the puzzle pieces click. Before you know it, you're coding!
