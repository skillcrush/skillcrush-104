Keep Breathing

Hey you have no idea what you're doing, what ruby is doing or anything else but you're still alive so things can't be that bad, right?

Read Around The Subject. Lots.

So Skillcrush is great but it moves pretty damn quickly and if you're new to programming languages (as I am) it would probably be a good idea to read up on ruby, get together some resources to fall back on and a good reference guide. Otherwise by week 4 you'll be totally lost and splashing around looking for help.

Like me.
