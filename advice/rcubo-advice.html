Hi my name is Skillcrush and I have some advice for you!

Learn best coding practices from the get go!

I learned how to code at university at first, but they were too deep into making us solve hard problems with code rather than learning the industry standards

Plus, in addition, the languages they use are often outdated! I know some people in CS learning Pascal!

And they don't teach git either...

Always learn a bit every day

It's easy to get absorbed into work, family and stuff.

However, if you improve just a little bit every day in what you do, you'll be a master eventually!
