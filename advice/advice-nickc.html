Hi, my name is Nick and I have some advice for you!

Practice, practice, practice

My main advice is to keep practicing at coding. You will fail. A lot. The only way I managed to improve was to keep working at it.

Look Stuff Up

There are many resources for coding online. Use them. Chances are that someone else has had the same problem or issue that you have had

Take Breaks

It might seem counterproductive, but whenever I feel like I can't figure out the solution to a problem, I take a break. It helps clear my head. I feel like I am able to look at the problem differently than I had before.
