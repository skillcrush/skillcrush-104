My name is Evelin and I've been learning to code for quite some time now. Here's some friendly advice to myself. To be read any now and then.

  1. Be kind to yourself. Look how far you've already come! CSS does no longer hold too many secrets to you. You communicate with your computer via Terminal. You're using version control. Be a little proud of yourself!
  2. Sometimes you will want to give up. Don't!
  3. Show off your skills any now and then. It's a great feeling to share your joy with others.
  4. If nothing seems to be working and you feel the urge to throw your computer out of the window. Fire up Terminal and type in the following command:
    say "It's not you, it's Javascript being mean again!"
