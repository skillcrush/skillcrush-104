Hi my name is Kayla

and I have some advice for you!

Perserverance

I can't even tell you how many times I wanted to throw in the towel on a project. Messed up something and can't find what's throwing it off. Or I'm trying to figure out the difference between, wrappers, or containers, or divs, and do I use a main or a section? Half the time I don't think I have any idea what I'm doing and I'm googling this or looking back through my skillcrush bookmarks and I feel like I'm in way over my head. But still, you just gotta keep going. It's part of the process.

Practice

But then I remember, at some point, all developers must have been there. It's like learning a new laguage, and at first you don't really know what you're saying, or if what you're saying is really the proper way to convery your message. But at a certain point, you just try, do your best, and keep practicing. Then something will click. You'll see it work, your point comes across, and it's a little easier next time. The more you do it, the more you start to see what works and what doesn't.

Play

Let's be real, as frusterating as it can be, when you finally get it, you feel like the smartest kid in the room and wish you could get a gold sticker... oh wait, is that just me? What was once a struggle now becomes a small, but mighty victory. Use that momentum to get you over the next wall and eventually this success is yours! Hooray! Hey, I think maybe I've got the hang of this thing (well, until my next test of perserverance comes along)!

This is a really fun process, because the creative possibilities are endless. Here, your dreams can be styled to your ♥'s content!

Play on, playa.
