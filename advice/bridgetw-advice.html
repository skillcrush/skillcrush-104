Gimme a Break

No, really.


When it comes to coding, breaks are amazing for problem solving.

Every time I ran up against a tricky problem that I couldn't solve, taking a day or two to pause from coding let me come back to the problem with fresh.

I am able to find a solution much faster and with less frustration than if I had try to force it.
