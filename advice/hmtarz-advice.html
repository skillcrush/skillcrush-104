Hi my name is H M and I have some advice for you!

Be patient with yourself!

When you start programming, you might feel stupid or confused at first. Do not let that feeling get you down!

The truth is that with practice, patience, and persistance, you too will be able to master coding. In the words of Elle Woods, "What, like it's hard?"

Precision, precision, precision

Your code is you speaking directly to the computer. This powerful device will precisely execute your requests, but you have to word them CLEARLY.

If you make a mistake in your code, the computer will not auto-correct it for you. You have to go back and ensure that every step, every letter, is correct and accurate.
