Hi my name is Jessica and I have some advice for you!

It will probably be a little uncomfortable!

Learning to code will most definitely be difficult at first. It is almost the same as learning a new language. Some days, the information will be easy to pick up. Other days, the information presented will be more difficult to understand and you may spend time struggling with little bits of information. Learning a new language can be a little painful at times. That usually will pass and you will end up better for having struggled. Onward and upward!

Confusion, therefore, is a sign that you are heading in the right direction. It's a signal that you have engaged in the problem solving pursuit that is math and technology and that it's time for you to dig deeper and keep going because clarity awaits.

Relax.
