Hi my name is Tylar and I have some advice for you!

Confusion is Correct!

When you start out coding or programing from never doing anything like it before your bound to be overwhelmed and confused. But that's ok! In fact that's normal!

Confusion, therefore, is a sign that you are heading in the right direction. It shows that you are headed in the right direction. If your thinking this isn't confusing or is too easy make sure you're doing it correctly.

Uncertainty is Ok!.

When learning a new trade or skill your bound to be uncertain at times.That is perfectly normal, heck even seasoned pro's get uncertain from time to time. Nothing is perfect, just like no one is perfect. And that is key!

This is a normal reaction to have.

Learning a new skill can be daunting, confusing, and a whole lot of uncertainty. But that is the beauty of coding. It's always going to be there! Don't be afraid to ask for help.
