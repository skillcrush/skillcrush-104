Keep it Simple

Regardless if this is the first time you are programming or if have experimented with it in the past. Keep it Simple. It took me way longer to do my milestone project as I felt that I needed to have it perfect and it had to do everything I invisioned my site to do. After realizing I was over complicating it, I decided that I had was enough to move to the next SkillCrush course. Looking back I wish I would have not overcomplicated things in and just stuck to the basics as there was a ton of time wasted. :( Don't let that happen to you! KEEP IT SIMPLE... well at least to start out :)
