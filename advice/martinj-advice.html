Hi my name is Martin Jarrin and I have some advice for you!

Here are the 5 tips when you starting to code!

  1. Be patient with yourself everyones learning styles and rythms are different, just focus on being consistent with your coding.
  2. When you get stuck on a problem, dont get discouraged , instead get excited knowing that each challenge is a potential learning opportunity.
  3. Have a vision that motivates you of why you are learning code, it will encourage you to put in the hard hours in the present.
  4. Dont be afraid of taking risks in order to expand your knowledge , get your feet wet and develope self initiative, it will make you a better problem solver.
  5. Use your mentors and teachers for guides, if you have already spent enough time on a problem and still cant solve it, do not keep wasting time.
