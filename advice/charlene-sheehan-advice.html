Hey I'm Charlene, and here's some advice for your coding adventure!

So many costs!

At first, this program seems expensive, but $200 a month is doable, right?!

Well, there's not only that. Don't forget the Adobe costs, and the website costs, and who know what else is left...

Don't screw yourself financially for this! Cut back on your personal expenses (bars, new clothes and uber - I'm looking at you). Work extra shifts if possible!

Keep paying for the program, but hold off on the recurring extra fees as long as you can if you can't afford it!

But most importantly...

don't give up!

You've already made it this far! It's frustrating at times, but we're going to make it. Keep working hard and your future in development is all yours!
