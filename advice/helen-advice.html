Hi my name is Helen and I have some advice for you!

Don't give up

Yes it's scary to be unemployed and trying to learn. But don't give up because of this! Getting a "temporary" job just makes it more difficult to learn to be able to get the job you actually want!

So, just do it. Suck it up for a few months. Be broke. Live small. Learn lots and poor your heart into it!

Use your resources

Don't be afraid to ask for help. Everyone started out as a noob at some point, as far away as it may seem. Everyone thinks differently, and might have different ways of solving youre problem.

Listen to what everyone has to say, whether you agree or not. Can't hurt to learn something new ;)
