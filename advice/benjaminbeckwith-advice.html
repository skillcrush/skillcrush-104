Embrace the Dead Ends and Getting Stuck

Learning new things can be exhilarating and exciting, and then at times frustrating and discouraging. Work through the dead ends and getting stuck and take careful time to truly learn and figure it out. The reward and encouragement of discovery and success are worth the struggle.
