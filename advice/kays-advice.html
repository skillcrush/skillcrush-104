Hi my name is Kay and I have some advice for you!

Be Patient!

When you start learning to code, a million thoughts will run in your mind. Will I be able to learn this? Will I ever be good enough to get a job? How can I learn everything? Your confidence level will hit an all time low, but you should never give up. There are many people out there feeling the same way. The truth is, you will never be able to know everything. Experienced developers today are still learning.

The key to becoming a developer is to be patient, don't give up, ask for help, and keep building projects. Over time, your confidence level increase and soon enough you will land that dream job of yours.
