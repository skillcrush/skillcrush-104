Hi my name is Lea Ann and I have some advice for you!

Use it or lose it!

Practice makes perfect...or at least makes you somewhat proficient.

Going through the challenges then taking the time to make up your own challenges is key to drilling in all that you have learned.

Resources, like Google and OneNote, are your friend!

Sometimes it's faster to google your problem than digging around in the resources you have amassed.

OneNote or another note taking tool with full text search is super helpful when you are in a jam.

Being married to a developer/IT guy helps as well. I just ask him. But then he tells me to Google it!!
