Believe In Yourself!

When you are learning something brand new and fairly complicated, like coding, it is extremely easy to tell yourself things like: "I'm just not cut out for this stuff," or "This is just crazy! What am I thinking?" or "I should just stick to what I know."

Well, I believe that everyone who ever began learning anything challenging has had those same thoughts and said those same things to themself.You are no different than they are! You have to remember that every time you sit down to study and/or practice, you are making progress. And, eventually, you WILL get to where you really want to be.

The difference between you, right now, and the people that are doing what you want to be doing is that they persevered through those self-defeating behaviors because they believed in themselves; and they knew that with time and effort, they would be successful. So, it becomes a choice, albeit a difficult one sometimes: Do I say f**k it and give up and be disappointed with the rest of my life? or Do I have faith in my efforts despite the sometimes frustrating learning process?

If it were easy, everyone would be doing it!
