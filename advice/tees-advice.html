How and Why

One of the mistakes I made the first time I learned to code was not understanind why something was working the way it was. I was so focused on figuring out how to get things to work, and never focused on why it worked that way. Understanding why and how something works allows you to truly know when to use certain technologies as well as how to extend those technologies if and when you need to.

Coding is a Jorney

Technologies is constantly changing. Being in a technology field means you become a life-long learner. You never get to a point where you've learned it all and can become complacent. Figuring out how to incorporate constant learning and exerpiementation is a key part of being a coder.

Have fun

Maybe the most important advice is to make sure your doing something that you enjoy. Taking the time to figure out what your passion is and invest in making a living doing that is one of lifes secrets. It's really really hard, but it's worth the soul searching and investment.
