Hi, my name is Evan and I have some advice for you!

Practice.

Coding is an art form, like painting or music. In order to best express yourself, you have to know how to use your tools. Just like learning to mix and apply color or play pleasing-sounding chords and melodies, you have to learn the language of code. And in order to do THAT, you have to practice. Theoretical understanding won't get your website built. You have to try out each and every line of code to make sure the computer is doing what you want it to do. And that's it!
Practice. Practice. Practice.
