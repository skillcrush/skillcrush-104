Hiya my name is Claire and I have some advice for you!

Don't compare

When I started, I kept comparing my progress and projects to other Skillcrushers. But this doesn't do any good! Everyone is working at their own pace and has their own style.

Keep working whenever you can. Even if you fall behind a little, just keep trucking along.

Celebrate the good days and work through the bad days.

I thought this whole process would be a bit challenging, but mostly exciting new steps every day. Some days I don't make it anywhere.

I make sure to pat myself on the back on the days that I tackle new mountains. And on bad days, I have to learn how to shake it off and pick myself back up.

You can't appreciate the good without the bad!
