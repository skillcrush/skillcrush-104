Hi, my name is Julia.
I have some advice for you.

Know thyself!

What are your personal strengths? Are you happier when creating than when consuming? Of course you are! Are you exceptionally organized - to the point where you may drive others crazy? Yes! Have you always inwardly cringed at the sight of Comic Sans? Haven't we all? Know these qualities & own them proudly! They'll serve you well in the tech field. And knowing them in advance may just be the encouragement you need when you find yourself in a particularly challenging situation! Write these down. Post them in a place you can see them. Remind yourself regularly how wonderful you are and why you're just the person for this job.

Just start typing!

So you have a complex design in front of you and you don't know where on earth to start? Just start with what you know. Add some content! Having something - anything - in place will give you that momentum you need to start tackling more challenging elements. Look at you go!

Phone a friend!

Feeling a little like you're drowning? Got a problem you just can't seem to fix? Just looking for a pat on the back for your sick new design? You're in the perfect position to collaborate. And you're probably in good company, too! Your fellow developers are not scary people - they value you as a creative and are more than happy to help! Reach out to them. No, really... Go, do it now!
