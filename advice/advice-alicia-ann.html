Hi my name is Alicia and I have some advice for you!

Confusion is your friend!

  • Codepen is awsome for quickly messing around with HTML, CSS, and Javascript
  • Starting to code is hard because you don't know what you don't know, ya know?
  • Having a mentor can really help with comprehension
  • Join a local meetup group so you can network and learn at the same time!
  • Sometimes it's best to take a break when you have an error, and come back with fresh eyes
