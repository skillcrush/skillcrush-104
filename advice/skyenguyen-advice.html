Hi there, my name is Skye Nguyen and I have some advice for you!

It's never too late to learn

Even now as I have started my journey, sometimes I regret not starting to code sooner. But then I remind myself that it's better be late to the game than to never be in the game!

Surround yourselves with like-minded people

Don't hesitate to reach out and organize meetings with people in your area who have the same interest as you. Having a support system is very important!
