Hi my name is Cheryl and I have some advice for you!

You are not alone

I think one of the most difficult challenges when learning to code is thinking you are the only one who doesn't "get it." And that's not true. Some people are just really good at hiding what they don't understand. I have found that it is better to admit you don't understand and...

ASK QUESTIONS

Just like we have all been there learning to read or tie our shoes, everyone in programming has been there learning to code. And, mostly, everyone remembers that and is willing to help those who are also learning and, mostly, the world of coding is a collaborative, cooperative place where people like to help other people learn.

Answer Questions

And when you are done learning the something you needed to learn, pay it forward by helping the next person learn it too. I've also learned that sometimes helping someone else makes you better at it because you have to learn how to explain it.
