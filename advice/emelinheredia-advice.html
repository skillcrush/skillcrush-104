Hi my name is Emelin and I have some advice for you!

Don't give up!

When you start programming it's like learning a new language. At times it could be really difficult and seem like you have no idea what you are looking at, but after practice and dedication you grow a love for it.

Ask, ask, ask!!

With coding comes confusion, but you're never alone. Ask your fellow skill crushers for help when needed! Don't be afraid to ask a "dumb" question. It's better to know how to do something right then to get frustrated and not do it right!.

Breathe!

When you are learning a new technology, it can be a big challange. There mgiht be times where you feel like quiting. Don't let your bad days get the best of you, just take a deep breathe and step away from your screen for a few minutes and get back to it!
