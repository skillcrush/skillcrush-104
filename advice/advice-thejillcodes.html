Hi, my name is Jillian and I have some advice for you!

There is an answer for every question.

It is easy to feel overwhelmed by unfamiliar jargon and inundated with solutions from online searches.

As a beginner in a tech field, you are lucky to have online resources and message board updated in real time. Find sites like Stack Overflow or WC3 and add not only the info to your working document, but a referral link to review future updates.

Once you find the learning style that suits you and you create a knowledge base, then it will be easy for you to not only become fluent but learn new programming languages.

Organize your processes along the way.

You are going to have to learn how to learn, and constantly check in with yourself.

Do not be scared to document successful processes in a language that you understand. In fact, these notes are crucial for continued development and problem solving. No one should have to search through open tabs and PDFs -- if you have to look something up, then you'd better include the solution in your working document!
