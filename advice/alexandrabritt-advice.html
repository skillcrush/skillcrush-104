Hi, my name is Alexandra Britt and I have some advice for you!

You can do this!

Programming is NOT easy. But once you get the hang of it, you'll start to love it!

Imposter syndrome is real!

You start doubting yourself and feel like you have no idea what you're doing. No matter how much you know it's never good enough.

I want you to know that this is normal. You're not alone. Actually most of us go through this but the good news is there are ways to fight self-doubt.

    Claim your title. This will help you feel more comfortable with this new identity.

    Share what you know with other people. You will not only prove to yourself that you DO have the skills but you will also learn more in the process

    Find a community of fellow coders. Once you realize that everyone dealt with self-doubt, no matter how successful they are, will give you a boost of confidence.

    And most importantly, TAKE CREDIT! Once you stop using those little phrases that downplay your knowledge, you will find yourself feeling more confident.
