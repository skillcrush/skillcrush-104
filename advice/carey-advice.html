here's some advice for you.

Know how to search

One of the first things I was ever taught when learning how to code is to know how to search. It was nice to know that even experienced coders have to ask for help on a regular basis.

You're learing a new language. It will take time.

My husband actually said this to me when I was getting frustrated with not knowing everything right away. "People get 4 - 6 year degrees in this. You're taking a three month course!"

It helped to put it in perspective.
