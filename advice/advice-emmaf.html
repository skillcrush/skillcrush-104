Hi my name is Emma and I have some advice for you!

Don't get Overwhelmed

Learning something new can seem overwhelming at first, but remember that practice makes perfect. Take it slow, use all the cheatsheets and resources that Skillcrush provides, do some research on your own, and repeat steps as much as needed

Don't forget to reach out to fellow Skillcrushers and schedule office hours.

Get up and Stretch.

It is very easy to lose track of time when you are coding. Make sure you set an alarm and stand up and stretch at least for five minutes every hour.

Drinking lots of water helps too!
