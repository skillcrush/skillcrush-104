Internet is your friend

Learning web development can be quite confusing, especially when you have to do it on your own. Don't be afraid to use online resources/forum or even Youtube. This is how I started learning about all the basics for programming and web design!

The next step would be to take an online course usually you have to pay for them. Signing up for Skillcrush was one of the best decision that I have made in web design! In less than 4 weeks, I learned so many great things.

My last advice would be to NEVER GIVE UP. Programming can be frustrating and confusing, but at the end of the day, you are creating something that speaks to you. I just love it!
