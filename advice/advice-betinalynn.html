Hi my name is Skillcrush and I have some advice for you!

Betina Lynn

When you start programming you are likely entering a world and an industry that is completely foreign to anything you have undertaken before.

Confusion, therefore, is inevitable .

Suspended disbelief plays an important role.

Trust the process. There will be many, many times when a new concept or process seems really difficult and no matter what you try, the result isn't turning out the way you think it should.

This is a normal. It is also normal to doubt yourself in this new world of coding and web development and wonder if you have what it takes to be successful.

Trust in yourself and keep moving forward. Remind yourself why you set out on this journey originally. Remember what you are working towards. You will have breatkthroughs when things suddenly start making sense where before it seemed like it was all gibberish.
