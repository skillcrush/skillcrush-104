It takes a village

  • Most of the work and learning you do will be your own hard work, time, energy, money, etc.
  • Don't fall into the trap that it's only you though. It's your family, friends, neighbors, colleagues. Everybody has a part in your journey.
  • Don't be afraid or ashamed to ask for help. There is no shame in receiving help, or not knowing something.
  • Don't be pig-headed. Be humble. Be grateful. Remember that everybody around you helped you attain your success.

The Power of Attraction

Like seeks like. If you are positive, industrious, helpful, and kind; those same qualities from others will find you.
Get rid of the negativity, the emotional vampires, the gossips, the jealous. They don't have an intrest in your success.

Don't look back. You aren't going that way.
