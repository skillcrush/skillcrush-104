Don't Give Up

It will all look so intimidating in the beginning but the hard work pays off. Learning these skills will not only take you far but also give you a huge confidence boost!

Networking Is So Important

Go to events and use the internet to find people to talk with about your journey. They will inspire you when you feel frustrated and can turn out to be good connections when looking for potential jobs.

Use Your New Skills For You

You can build so many cool things with code. Make sure you are doing it for you in order to gain emotional income alongside financial income $$$$$$$
