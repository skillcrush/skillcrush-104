Hello! My name is Dugancodes and I have some advice for you!

You will want to pull your hair out during this journey but don't quit!

I have had many evenings during the last three months where I just wanted to quit. It's too hard! I am too old to figure this out! I am never going to learn this!

I had no developer skills. I have been an auditor for 28+ years. I needed a change in my life. Call it a mid-life crisis!


Keep plugging away at this because it will be worth it!

I have a new appreciation for all of the work that goes into the websites that I visit every day. I had no idea what it took to design and develop code for a website.

I am amazed by what I have learned in three months! At age 50! You can teach a "older" dog new tricks! Who knew? Thank you Skillcrush!
