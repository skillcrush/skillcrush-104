Hi my name is Renee and I have some advice for you!

There are no dumb questions!

Always remember there is only one way to learn and that is by asking questions.

In order to learn we need to fail, embrace the learning process.

Perseverance and determination are key to your success.

No matter how hard it looks or how stuck or confused you feel you will eventually find an answer to the challenges you encounter.

When this happens take a break and re-look at the problem at a later time. Most of the times all we need is a fresh perspective.

The only way to become an expert at something is by practicing.
