Never to late

No matter what age a career in Tech is not out of reach. Since there are a lot of remote and freelance jobs available, not one can tell the age behind the screen.

Be sure to practice everyday to make sure you sharpen your skills

You will never learn everything!

There are so many new programs that there is no way to learn it all. Things change all the time so you are always learning.

This is a normal part of the process of learning a new technology, and in fact, it's a critical part of the process.

If it it something that you really want, you can do it!!
