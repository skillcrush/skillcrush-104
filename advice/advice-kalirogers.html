Baby Steps

On my very first website client, I took on more than I should have. I programmed it in html and css then turned it into a wordpress theme. It took me FOREVER! I look back now and feel like I should have taken smaller steps. Focused on learning one new skill with each project instead of diving into the deep end head first.

More time equals more practice

Time has been a valuable thing I have given myself. I took the wordpress blue print and then a year later took the front end developer blueprint. I think the time has allowed me to focus on what I like and what I want to learn next. I haven't been overwhelmed during the learning process becasue I have taken my time through each step.

Enjoy the process

Everyone says that right! Ha. But for real, I have enjoyed learning something new and that is what keeps me coming back to coding. I love that there is alwasy something to learn.
