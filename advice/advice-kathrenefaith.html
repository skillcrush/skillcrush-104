Hi my name is Kathrene and I have some advice for you!

Practice is perfect.

Keep practicing coding PSDs to HTML and CSS. Allot time for it! With that said...

Keep to a consistent schedule.

Like learning any new skill, you need discipline to refine your ability. This one is especially hard for me, because I'm a natural go-with-the-flow kind of person.

Confidence is key.

Be confident in your own progress and don't compare yourself to others. Your journey in coding is your own. Remember this, especially if you are working a fulltime job and juggling other responsibilities besides learning how to code!

Have GRIT.

No matter the setbacks, the discouragement (from others or self-inflicted), keep pursuing your learning. Think of the breadth of possibilities -- creative and career-related -- that will be made available to you because you can code! Eyes on the prize, baby.
