Hi my name is Crystal, designer extraordinaire, and I have some advice for you!

Just Go!

There's so many resources available that it can be overwhelming at times to know just where to begin. At times, I ended up with analysis paralysis where I was analyzing available resources to the point I wasn't actually learning anything. If I could go back I would tell myself to just start somewhere, even if I ended up taking a different direction later on. Just GO!

When in doubt, ask!

As mentioned before, there are so many resources available, if you have a questions it's most likely been asked before. All you have to do is ask, search, Google, etc. you're bound to find an answer

Celebrate the Wins

There may be times you don't think you're getting anywhere in the process. Keep a journal or notes on what you've worked on, you'll be surprised how quickly things start to build. Soon you'll be able to look back 2 weeks, 2 months, a year and see how far you've come
