Hi my name is Adrianne and I have some advice for you!

Don't give up!

When you start programming your first website, you may run into problems you can't imagine solving. Don't give up. Look for help. Ask for help. A fresh set of eyes can also see the error more clearly.

Remember to take care of yourself!

When you are learning a new subject, give yourself a break! You probably have other responsibilities or children who may not allow you to have the focus you need. Be kind to yourself and remember your why!
