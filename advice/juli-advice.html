Hi my name is Juli and I have some advice for you!

Code Every Day!

Don't compare your journey to other's.

Don't quit asking questions.

Don't give up when you don't understand and your head is spinning and you just want to kick a chair...just step away. Some times distance from a problem allows clairty to surface. The phrase "sleep on it" doesn't come from no where.

Don't ever allow yourself to feel stupid. You aren't. Coding is hard and it takes patience and determination - no matter what the hacker says.

Do enjoy the ride.
