Hi my name is Larissa and I have some advice for you!

Just keep going!

Getting started on a project is hard.

Tell yourself you're going to work for 5 minutes. Those 5 minutes are way less stressful than coding an entire website.

This will at least get you going.

And even if you don't want to do more than 5 minutes, at least you'll have done something.

But I bet once you get to the 5-minute mark, you'll keep going.
