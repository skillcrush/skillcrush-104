Hi my name is Lisa and I have some advice for you!

Making mistakes is acceptable!

When you start coding, you are going to make mistakes. But do not worry! It is completely acceptable.

Part of the Learning Experience

Making mistakes is how we learn and it's the same with coding or programming. By making mistakes, especially in a development environment, you learn to troubleshoot your own code

You will learn to research problems that you may not know the answer to which in turn, allows you to experiment with new things.

The great thing about coding, is that you can always fix your mistakes. The next time you make a mistake that required a bit of troubleshooting on your part, be sure to document your mistake and the solution. This will also help you remember how to prevent the mistake the next time.
