Don't Get Frustrated

You probably won't get it right on the first try every time.

If you feel stuck, walk away for ten minutes and come back with a fresh approach.

Don't be afraid to make mistakes.

Learning how to really read your code to fix your mistakes is one of the best ways to develop your skills.

Don't be afraid to reach out for help!
