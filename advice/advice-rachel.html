Hi my name is Rocky and I have some advice for you!

Just Keep Going

You'll get a feeling at a certain point that the information is coming faster than you can process it. Just keep going. It will all come together eventually.

Practice. Practice. Practice.

There is so much to learn! And everything connects to everything else. That means that you need to practice what you learn as much as possible. The more you practice what you've already learned, the easier it will be to add new information on top of it.
