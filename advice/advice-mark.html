Advice From codebymark


Learn one thing at a time. You'll drive yourself crazy trying to learn Ruby, Python, Javascript and PHP at the same time. Choose a path, master it and then move on to something new.


Ask LOTS of questions! However consider researching and trying something first, then you can talk about your experience and what you know so far with the person you're asking.


When you get stuck and you will, get away from your computer, go outside, take a nap or email yourself a description of the problem to read later.
