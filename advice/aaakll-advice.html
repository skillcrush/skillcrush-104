Double, Triple and Quadruple Check!

When your code doesn't work it can be extremely frustrating. And it's always due to user error. Inputing the correct commands and keys is ESSENTIAL

Cut Yourself Some Slack!

Good for you for learning something new! It's not easy! Don't get down on yourself if it doesn't come naturally or work the first time.

I have found, when I hit a roadblock and start to become super frustrated, if I step away and do something else, I'm able to reboot my brain and fix it when I come back to it.
