Hi, my name is Deborah and I have some advice for you!

Keep persevering!

Learning to code is hard but don't give up. You may not grasp some of the concepts straight away but keep plugging away. And sometimes you just need to take a little break and when you come back to the problem the solution becomes clear

Use your resources!

Google, classmates, podcasts, local meet-ups - use them all. It's ok to ask for help.

Take notes!

Taking notes helps you to retain information. Take time to go back over the lessons even though its tempting to speed through everything.
