Hi my name is Emily and I have some advice for you!

Find YOUR focus time.

For me that means Getting Up Early!! When you have a busy schedule and are constatly pulled in multiple directions, find the time of day that you can best focus in and be productive.

Since I've been waking up to do Skillcrush before I go to work in the mornings, my drive has skyrocketed since I actually feel accomplished when I sit down to work through lessons and projects. Find the time of day that works best for your schedule and energy flow!

Ignore the Haters and Negativity. Listen to your Gut and Stay Focused on your Goals.

Consider the advice of friends, loved ones and people experienced in the field, but don't let any negativity get in your way. Even if it's coming from someone you really care about.

Remember your intention and goals, stay flexible, and just keep swimming!!
