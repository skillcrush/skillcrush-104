Hi, my name is Corinne and I have some advice for you!

You can figure anything out!

Even if something seems completely out of your realm of understanding, just keep putting one foot in front of the other.

You never know when you're going to discover how to do something new. And it feels so amazing when it clicks!

Ask for help AFTER you've tried everything.

Even though you may feel alone at your computer, you're not.

Everyone was a beginner once. And most people enjoy helping others.

After you've tried your best, reach out and ask for help. You'll be amazed at how generous people are with their knowledge and experience.
