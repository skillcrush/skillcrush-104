Hi my name is Rachel and I have some advice for you!

Don't be afraid to ask for help!

There are so many ways to get help on Skillcrush that help is always just around the corner.

Ask your question on Slack, via e-mail or chat, or in office hours. The instructors are super friendly and never make you feel dumb.

Trust in the process!

Sometimes you might feel like the lessons are too easy and that you can skip ahead. Sometimes you might feel like things are too hard and you don't know why you're doing what you're doing.

You might start to feel frustrated, fearful, or wonder why the heck you signed up for this class anyway.

However, everything will come together, and with time and commitment you will be on your way to a rewarding career in tech!
