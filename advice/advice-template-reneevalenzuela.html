Hi my name is Renee and I have some advice for you!

Don't be afraid to start!

It all seems confusing at first, but once you start to tackle coding, it' will start to make sense.

I'm not saying it's all perfectly understandable when you first start the journey. But as long as you keep at it, the more you will understand. The puzzle pieces will start to fit together!

Practice, practice, practice!

Like any new endeavor, you need to practice, practice, practice. And ask lots of questons. Things will start to make sense after a while. Promise.

This is a normal part of the process of learning a new technology, and in fact, it's a critical part of the process.

You see when you encounter a new programming language part of your learning process includes learning to train your eye. The way to train your eye is to read a good deal of code even when you don't totally understand what it's doing.
