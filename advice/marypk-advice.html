Hi my name is Mary and I have some advice for you!

Never stop learning!

Once you feel like you have a base knowledge of website development, enough to create your own website, you may feel like "this is it". Don't ever stop there - continue to push yourself and continually strive for growth.

There are times when you feel like you might know "enough", but there will always be opportunities out there, either new clients or different projects, where a skill may be lacking. Continue to strive for that growth.

Continually invest in yourself!

It's important to find different avenues for growth. Investing in yourself doesn't always mean spending money on courses. It could be investing time & energy. Whatever you think you need to do to become a better version of yourself, really believe that if you invest time, or money, or energy, it will all be worth it
