Hi my name is Lesley and I have some advice for you!

Do not fear failure!

Some people can be set back by a fear of failure,

that it's too late for them to learn code, it's too hard, or that they won't be good at it.

I say face the fear and do it anyway!

With failure comes growth.

The master has failed more times than the novice has tried.
