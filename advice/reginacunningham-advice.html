Hi my name is Regina and I have some advice for you!

You will feel overwhelmed at times

Don't give up, keep on learning and coding. It gets better and you will feel more comfortable the more you practice.

Celebrate all of your victories and accomplishments, even the small ones.

There will be times you want to tear your hair out, but you'll figure it out, when you do celebrate.

It's not easy.

Learning tech skills is not easy, especially if you're a complete newbie. Don't let that discourage you.
