Keep Going

There will be times when you want to quit but just keep going. If you get really stuck, take a walk, read a book, talk to a friend, forget about the code.

When you come back to it, chances are that the problem that seemed so big to you before will stand out to you, and it will likely be just a misspelling or missing semi-colon.
