Hi my name is Kori and I have some advice for you!

Walk Away

No I am not saying completely walk away from coding forever. If you find yourself in a state of utter confusion, reach out

with a post in the forum explaining your dilema and then, walk away. Take some time to get something to eat, sleep or maybe g

grab an adult beverage. Don't stay away too long, but when yo return with a fresh outlook, everything will make more sense.
