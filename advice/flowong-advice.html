Hello.

My name is Flo,
and boy do I have some advice for you!

Have a growth mindset, not a fixed one.

Everyone starts off as newbies. Steve Jobs knew nothing about how computers worked, when he was a baby.

Even if you aren't now, you can be the next Ada Lovelace, with time, effort, and a name change.

Nothing is impossible.

Treat everything like a fun challenging puzzle -- don't know how that Javascript works? Google it. Not sure how to fix that error? Google. Need advice on your Tinder affairs? Google.

Google knows everything, until it doesn't.
