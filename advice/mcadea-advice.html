Hi my name is Michelle and I have some advice for you!

Learning is a Journey!

This is all new, the first time you are seeing code and software development.

You may feel overwhelmed at times. You can take breaks and come back to the material with fresh eyes.

The most important thing is that you are learning.

Lean into the Discomfort

At first you may not understand what the code is doing. After a while though, you will begin to see how the code works.

You see when you encounter a new programming language part of your learning process includes learning to train your eye. The way to train your eye is to read a good deal of code even when you don't totally understand what it's doing.
