Hi my name is Martin and I have some advice for you!

Don't Reinvent The Wheel

There is very little that is truly new under the sun. When you encounter a challenge, assume that someone else has come up with a solution. Then, just find it.

Youtube Is A Great Search Engine

The other day I needed to take some good photos of chocolate for our artisan chocolate business. So, I searched Youtube for tutorials about food photography and voila - everything from lighting to composition and food styling!

When All Else Fails, Sleep On It

Often, when trying to solve a technical problem, I find myself going round in circles, getting nowhere. I sense that I may be making an assumption that is wrong. It's amazing how, the next morning, you can look at things from a new direction.
