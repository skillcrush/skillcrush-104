Hi my name is Danja and I have some advice for you!

Don't give up and take yourself seriously!

It's going to be confusing and frustrating especially if you have no computer background like I did. But take yourself serious, don't undermine your determination and talent.

I take way too long to take myself seriously with anything I start on. I always think that I am not good enough yet. Do not make that mistake. Just dive in and try things. Trust yourself.

Be Curious!

Be curious and try new things. Have some side projects that you can escape to if you're getting worn out with the course or just need a change of work for a minute to refresh.

And most importantly: Don't worry! You'll get there. Just be patient and stick with it.

Now go and get back to coding! Good luck!
