Hi my name is Lungowe and I have some advice for you!

Taking the first step is all it takes!

So I finally took the plunge to learn how to code. After years of avoiding it like the plague, believing that I wasn't smart enough to do, I've realised that all I needed was to take the first step. And I'm glad I did!!

What inspired me the most was Adda's story of how she first got started in Tech. After I read her story, I immediately signed up for the Skillcrush Front-End Developer blueprint and I haven't looked back since.

Fight the Fear

Fear is inevitable as one embarks on a new journey but don't let it hold you back. Take a glimpse at how far you've come from and what you knew then and use that to compare the skills that you've acquired in the present. Naturally, you've come a long way. Always remember that and use it as a fuel to drive you even further.
