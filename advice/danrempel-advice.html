Hi my name is Dan and I have some advice for you!

Code every day!

Even if you don't know what you're coding. Just find a tutorial and follow it. After about ten of them you'll begin to recognize patterns and remember them. You'll eventually realize that everytime you create a new view you have to update the routes. Or Adding basic logic to your models.

I wrote an article about this called "Gaining Context: Hack Your Learning" on my blog, Rempel on Rails
