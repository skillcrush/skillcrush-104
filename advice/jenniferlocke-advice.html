Hi, my name is Jen; I have some advice for you!

Coding is hard...

But not as hard as you think. And sometimes harder than you think. Yes! I know that's confusing.

That's because some things will come to you easily. And others will give you problems. It's those times when you want to remember to use your resources. If you have books, you can check those. Cheat sheets? Bingo! Also, Google! And an unlimited number of websites dedicated to coding and web development.

Sometimes the "why" and "how" isn't readily apparent.

If you're like me, you need to know the reasons for things before they make sense. And sometimes you'll have to look at the sample code to figure out the why and the how. It's okay. Be patient with yourself and use your resources. See above.
