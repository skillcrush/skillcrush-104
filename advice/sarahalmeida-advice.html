Hi, my name is Sarah and I have some advice for you!

It's okay to feel discouraged at times.

When I first started programming I was full of worries and fears. Fear that I wasn't smart enough to learn something so complex and worry that my code wasn't good enough.

Ask for help when you need it.

What helped me was reaching out to the community in our google groups and beyond. The development community is amazing and everyone is super supportive!

Remember everyone was a beginner at one point.

Don't get hung up when your website doesn't look as fancy as someone else's. It's your website and you made it! That's a huge accomplishment in itself. Take a moment and reflect on how much you have learned and then continue on with confidence. You got this!
