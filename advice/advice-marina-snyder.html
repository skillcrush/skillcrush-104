Hi my name is Marina and I have some advice for you!

Confusion is your friend!

Start off working with people you know. It takes off some of the pressure.

Having a separate ergonomic chair for coding is great. When you code in bed it's harder to sleep and harder to code.

Work on projects

This is the fun part! Learning code all day and not putting it to use is like cooking for hours on end and not eating any of it.

Tea and background music make whatever room you're coding in feel less like a cave. Yes, to the outside world it may seem like I've been hiding in one, but that doesn't mean it should feel like it!

Backup all your work and expect to make mistakes. A lot of them. But those are the things that you'll remember how to fix the next time around.
