My name is Katie Gustafson & I have some advice for you!

Find the right path

If you are starting out as a complete beginner like I was, I can't stress enough how important it is to find something that works for you. For me that meant, after trial and error, realizing I needed a specific, guided class to follow with deadlines and projects. I also needed to learn from someone that would explain all the little things so I really understood. I wanted a full explanation of the basics so I could build from there. Find what works for you!

People that are willing to answer your questions are key!

It's important to know how to use online resources and tools available to coders to figure things out, but sometimes, especially in the beginning, having a person willing to answer some basic questions makes your understanding much smoother.

Having someone to say, "Look, you've almost got it" or "You're not too far off here" was an invaluable part of my confidence boosting and that's half the battle, right?

Don't forget to use all the tools in the toolbox - people, guides, online resources, meetups, the list goes on. This journey is much better with support!
