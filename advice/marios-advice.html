Hi my name is Mario and I have some advice for you!

Coding takes plenty of patience and time, but can be mastered!

At first when you start programming you think you've entered a journey that never ends. After we get the basics though I feel like it's very straightforward.

In my opinion being a good programmer is knowing how to do good Google searches and looking for help when you need it. Finding the right resources out there is key!

Steps to take when starting out to see if you have the patience and right mindset for coding.

Look up free resources on Google like Codecademy, FreeCodeCamp, GA Dash and etc. to see if you dig it and go through some tutorials. :)

If you like it and feel like you would love to do it as a career start looking into some paid training. Sign up for a coding bootcamp and complete it. :)

Your last step would surely be to go hunt for a job in the city or work on your own.
