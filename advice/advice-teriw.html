Hi, my name is Teri and I have some advice for you!


Desire is Your Gift!

Skillcrush has opened my eyes to the many facets of desire. I never dreamed that desire would play such a significant part in shaping a new me! Learning how to code has been a real eye-opening experience! As I look back on my experience I can see how I was paralyzed by my own fears to embrace change. I now see how important it is to be less rigid toward how we think or do things and more open to being flexible in adapting to the evolving technology field.

Desire is the Key

Desire, then, is the key in shaping your destiny. I've learned from the powerful truth given in orientation, viewing master class videos, and interacting with instructors and classmates what to anticipate.
Some sage advice that I've learned is that persistence pays, positive thinking is powerful, keep it simple, and celebration is exhilarating!

Therefore, if you have a question,don't be afraid to ask. If you're beginning to get angry, be happy. If you're ordinarily an organized rather than random thinker, read between the lines.
Lastly, celebrate that if you're reading this,"the light at the end of the tunnel isn't the illusion--the tunnel is".

The Gift of Desire

I've learned that desire is...adaptive, purposeful, persistent, persevere, intentional, deliberate, and strategic. Such a gift!
Congrats and crush on until you get your miracle!
