Hi there! I'm Kristiana and I have some advice for you!

Make time for happiness! :)


Do things that make you happy. Small or big. Eat that cookie, go on that hike, watch that movie, take that vacation! Just have fun, but be safe.

Find something you enjoy doing. :D


Just imagine something you'd like to do and make it happen. Perhaps programming is one of those things!

Spend time alone & with others. ^_^


Time is too short to spend it all alone. Meeting new people is a great experience. Being with the ones you care about is also just as wonderful.
