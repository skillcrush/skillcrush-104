Hi my name is Melissa and I have some advice for you!

Taking classes twice really helps you retain the information.

The first time you take a class is an introduction to the concepts. The second time you take the class, the information and lessons sink in and make sense.

Follow the class schedule.

The best way to learn is to do each lesson on the day it's assigned. It makes the class work manageable and less stressful.

Practice what you have learned.

Practicing what you have learned will ensure you don't forget it.
