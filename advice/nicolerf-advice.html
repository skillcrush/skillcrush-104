Hi my name is Nicole, and I have some advice for you!

Don't Let The Bugs Bug You :)

As you learn to code, remember that errors and troubleshooting are a huge part of the fun! The time you spend on challenging (frustrating) problems is also where your skills and expertise can grow the most. Enjoy investigating and take every obstable as an opportunity to dig deeper into the details of your craft.

Also keep in mind - Even the most impossibly challenging dilemmas have solutions. As you stick with it, technological difficulties become less cause for alarm, and more approachable as a regular part of doing web development. Problem solving in development is a creative and rewarding process!

Hey Sleuth, Did I Mention Techies Are Super Sharing?

The tech/development/design community is awesome! Tons of reliable and free info is available to you online via GitHub, Stack Overflow, along with many many other open resources. Chances are, you'll find similar questions to yours along with a variety of detailed solutions.

Coding is a fantastic skill to have. Starting your journey in tech will open you up to a multitude of professional opportunities, and provide skills applicable to all kinds of career paths and interests.

Stay sharp and have as much fun with the bugs as you can!
