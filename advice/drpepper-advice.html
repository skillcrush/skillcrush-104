Hi my name is Alex and I have some advice for you!

Do not think this will be easy.

Coding can be a lot of fun. But in no way is it easy to understand. There are more than a few steps it takes to figure things out in the tech world.

Once you understand something after much frustration is very cool. A smile breaks out and you're like, "Wow I did this. Go me!"

This will be frustrating.

The frustration coding has brought me is almost unbearable. It makes me want to give up almost everyday and go live on a remote kiwi farm in New Zealand. But I don't. Because I know I can learn and so can you. It just takes a lot of practice and...maybe a drink at the end of the day.

Just think. If it was easy to do this for a living then everyone would do it!

Always remember that there are all kinds of different programming. There's no telling what you'll be able to do once you find the right language and developing inspiration.
