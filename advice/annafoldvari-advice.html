Don't Give Up

The most important thing is that just keep doing it. Nobody says learning to code is easy. There will be times when you just want to stop it and not code anymore. Don't do it. The secret of success is grit according some very wise people. So if you don't give up and keep practicing from day to day, then eventually you will get there. You will become an awesome programmer.

You don't have to be a genius to succeed.

Common misbelief you have to be super smart in this field to land amazing roles. However according to seasoned practitioners this is not true. You don't have to be a genius to be a good programmer. Just keep doing it and you will get there.
