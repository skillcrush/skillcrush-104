Hi my name is Melissa, and I have some advice for you!

It's never too late to start again

Even though I was obsessed with coding and design as a teenager, I took a 15-year gap before I realized that I wanted to pursue a career in front-end development.

Now in my 30s, I thought it was too late to transition to tech and that I would never be able to catch up. But I was wrong! So, if you're in the same position and having the same thoughts, just know that it's NOT too late.

Skillcrush makes it easy!

Finding this program changed everything for me, and has given me the confidence to completely change careers.

You just need to find a learning method that works best for you, and stick with it! Spend all of your free time practicing, learning, and practicing some more.

Every new component that you learn and master will make you feel so accomplished, so stick with it!
