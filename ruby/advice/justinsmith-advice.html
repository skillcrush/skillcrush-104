Hi my name is Justin and I have some advice for you!

Take things piece by piece

The idea of making an entire webpage/app can be pretty intimidating. Instead of trying to attack the whole thing at once, it's a great idea to break it up into smaller pieces. This way you're only having to attack one bit by one bit. With each piece you conquer, you get closer to finishing the big picture, and after a while, the whole thing is done.
