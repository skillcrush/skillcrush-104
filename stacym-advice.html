"Hi, I'm Stacy and I have some advice for you!"

Frustration is inevitable.

Learn to like it because being stuck when learning to code or simply learning something that you haven't learned YET is vital to the process of learning it!

Learn to change your reaction to feeling lost. Instead of letting fear or even embarassment take over, tell those characters to take a seat. You are on a journey and they're not welcome. It's ok to enjoy being in the woods - you'll discover all kinds of things if you look around instead of panicing and only fixating on finding the way out.
